Nekfeu "On verra": a musical masterpiece

ONLINE 04/10/2022 at 7:24 p.m.

Adrien Leveque

"We will see" is the 11th title of the album "Feu". The single released on May 1, 2015 is the music that will mark the beginning of his rise on the French rap scene, it is also the title of the most "known" artist and will be a real success when it is released in summer 2015 .

This single will earn him the diamond certification of SNEP as well as a cumulative 80 million views on Youtube. Nekfeu will make a clip in which Mister V and Malika Ménard will make their appearances.

A thoughtful music

In his music, Nekfeu delivers a criticism above all societal as well as the passage of time, it is a form of self-questioning on his own actions. Several themes are recurrent such as love, drugs and alcohol or time.

Its lyrics are deep, it gives us emotions like few songs can do, it brings us positivity. It's also a real life lesson, he shows us the importance of enjoying life, because we only have one and it would be stupid to waste it, he never stops to remind us of this throughout his work.

Listen to it now

His lyrics have often been praised for their strong meaning and prodigious writing. They transmit a timeless message, indeed they were topical 7 years ago and still are today. "We'll see" also has a soothing power that makes it unique.

A criticism split in two

This music has caused a lot of debate and has divided the community of the artist, some consider this single as a musical masterpiece while others find that it does not reflect the true talent of the artist and have been strongly disappointed. Be that as it may, we can say that this music has worked very well until today and that it still has a bright future ahead of it.
