Laziness: A phenomenon on the rise among young people

ONLINE 04/10/2022 at 19H24

Adrien Leveque

In recent years, there has been a growing phenomenon of laziness among young people. This is particularly evident in developed countries, where young people have easy access to technology and can lead have easy access to technology and can lead a relatively comfortable life. comfortable life.

There are several reasons for this

There are a number of reasons why laziness is on the rise among youth. One reason is that young people are under more pressure than ever before. With the rise of social media and the 24-hour news cycle, young people are constantly bombarded with information and notifications. It can be overwhelming, and it's easier to stay in bed and bed and scroll through social media than to face the day.

Another reason for the rise of laziness among young people is that they have fewer responsibilities than previous generations. With the advent of telecommuting, many young people can work from home and set their own hours. This can lead to a sense of entitlement, and young people may feel that they don't need to work hard because they are not being supervised face-to-face by a superior.

Finally, laziness among young people can also be a result of the rising cost of living. In many developed countries, the cost of housing education, and health care have risen faster than wages.
