Filmography

  • Buddy - 2003

    Released in 2003, Buddy is Morten Tyldum's directorial debut, from a screenplay by Lars Gudmestad. He will win two Amanda price in 2004.

  • Fallen Angels - 2008

    Released in 2003, Fallen Angels is the second film by this same director and it is the fourth film adaptation of the series of detective novels written by Gunnar Staalesen.

  • Headhunters - 2011

    Headhunters is a Norwegian-German thriller film directed by Morten Tyldum and released in 2011. It is the adaptation of the novel Headhunters from Jo Nesbø.

  • Imitation game - 2014

    Imitation Game is a 2014 American biographical film directed by Morten Tyldum. It is the film adaptation of Alan Turing's biography from Andrew Hodges.

  • Passenger - 2016

    Passengers is a 2016 American science fiction film directed by Morten Tyldum.

  • Morten Tyldum

    Morten Tyldum is a Norwegian director, born May 19, 1967 in Bergen in the county of Hordaland. All his films will be more or less successful despite his limited number.
