About

Currently a student in the MMI department of the IUT of Belfort-Montbéliard, I am working on our first project “Notes for later”.

The goal of this project is to design a website in order to be consulted in several years and thus act as a kind of time capsule. This project thus makes it possible to understand the approach of design and web development.

I chose to go in a style with colors that can recall the era of the 80s, the logo of the site also supports this idea with an old-fashioned design.
