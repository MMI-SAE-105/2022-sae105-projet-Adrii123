Book extract

To derive the maximum benefit from this book, one essential quality is needed, more important than all the principles. Without this fundamental disposition, the best precepts would be of no use to you. But, if you have it, you will do wonders without reading these suggestions.

What is this magical condition? Simply this: the deep and irresistible desire to improve yourself, the desire to learn to get along better with those around you.

How can you develop this desire? Keeping constantly in mind the importance of the principles recommended here to you. Picture the happier life you will lead by applying them. Repeat to yourself tirelessly: “My happiness, my success, my reputation depend in large part on the talent that I will be able to deploy in relations with my fellow men.”
